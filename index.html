What is it?

Graphic design is the practice of composing and communicating ideas visually

(We design digtital & analogue things for people to see)

We solve problems!

Students explore these relationships through creative play/experimentation to help understand and develop their visual communication.

You will investigate Graphic design in the broadest sense, considering it in relation to:


  • Critical Studies (Theory)
  • Graphic Communication (You and the client)
  • Visual Expression (Your own creativity)
  • Design Fundamentals (Skills and Industry links)


All within a creative, design and technology ethos.
