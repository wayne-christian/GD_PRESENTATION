What is it?

Graphic Design is the practice of composing and communicating ideas visually

(We design digital & analogue things for people to see)

We solve problems!

You will explore these relationships through creative play & experimentation to help understand and develop your visual communication.

You will investigate Graphic Design in the broadest sense, considering it in relation to 4 modules:


  • Critical Studies (Theory)
  • Graphic Communication (You and the client)
  • Visual Expression (Your own creativity)
  • Design Fundamentals (Skills and Industry links)

All within a creative, design and technology ethos.
